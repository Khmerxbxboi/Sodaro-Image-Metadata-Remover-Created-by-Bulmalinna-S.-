Why this matters

Your photos are yours. Sodaro removes hidden metadata (e.g., GPS and device IDs) locally in your browser—nothing is uploaded—so you can share images without exposing your location or identity.

  • Protects privacy and location safety
  • Supports free expression without unwanted tracking
  • Respects freedom of association and movement online

Bottom line: Tools like Sodaro help keep our constitutional rights meaningful in the digital world.
